Why We Began Live Concerts

Many of our volunteers have greatly missed in-person performances for seniors, so we've looked for the next best thing: live recordings of concerts. We started in February 2021, a few months after the Tacy Foundation launched service learning at home during the pandemic, and we've been hosting events weekly ever since. Join us for one on a Saturday afternoon at 3:00 p.m., hosted by a Live Concerts staff member! We welcome all skill levels and instruments, as long as your performance work is in the public domain and ranging from 2 to 6 minutes, although if time permits we can accomodate longer works. If you're interested in performing, please fill out the form. We're glad to have you join us!

We utilize a platform called Streamyard to bring our performances to YouTube livestreams, and we distribute recordings of the livestream to senior living homes throughout the DMV area where we used to perform in person. One of the great benefits of this platform is that our performers don't need to sign up for any accounts. They can join a link to the "broadcast studio" and perform! We were inspired by the NSO @ Home performances to set up such virtual performances in our community.

If you'd like to watch our performances, visit our YouTube channel. Be sure to like, comment, and subscribe!
